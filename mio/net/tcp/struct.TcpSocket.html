fn connect(&self, addr: &SocketAddr) -> MioResult<bool>

Connects the socket to the specified address. When the operation completes, the handler will be notified with the supplied token.

The goal of this method is to ensure that the event loop will always notify about the connection, even if the connection happens immediately. Otherwise, every consumer of the event loop would have to worry about possibly-immediate connection.
